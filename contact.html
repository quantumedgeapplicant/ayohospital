Ayo Specialist Hospital

Ayo Specialist Hospital is a one-stop healthcare provider adhering strictly to industry best practices. It is located in Ibadan the capital of Oyo State and accessible from many strategic locations. Our team of professionals in different speciality of medicine work to provide patient-centred care to all. Self-motivated personnel care for patients with empathy, dignity, compassion and respect and so make it possible for our patients to choose to stay with us even at first visit. Historically, Ayo Specialist Hospital was established in 1985 as a small ambulatory clinic. Later, admission facilities/in-patient care were added.
